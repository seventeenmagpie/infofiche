Useful Resources

This page is effectively a reading list of explicitly linguistic SFF and also non-fiction that I've found useful in the course of writing this blog. If its linked to, then I've read and written about it.

Fiction:

  • Battle of the Linguist Mages by Scotto Moore,

  • A Half-Built Garden by Ruthanna Emrys,

  • *Barrier* by Brian K. Vaughan, Marcos Martin and Muntsa Vicente,

  • *A Memory Called Empire* by Arkady Martine (2019),

  • The Story of your Life by Ted Chiang,

  • Native Tongue by Suzette Haden Elgin,

  • Triton by Samuel R. Delany,

Non-fiction:

(A lot of these are TBR, and for SFF criticism, not just linguistics)

  • *Aliens and Linguists* by Walter E. Myers,

  • The Jewel Hinged Jaw by Samuel R. Delany,

About

Infofiche is a blog about science fiction, fantasy and linguistics. Made by Seventeen Magpie and sparked by Arkady Martine's A Memory Called Empire. I post on the nth day of the nth month, where n is each of the natural numbers from 1 to 12.
